# COMOT (Come Out)

## Executive Summary
Based on data from the Central Statistics Agency, unemployment in Indonesia was recorded at 8.75 million unemployed in February 2021, which was dominated by SMK graduates, which amounted to 11.45%. 

The impact of COVID-19 is one factor in the number of unemployed in Indonesia. Indonesian Ministry of Manpower found 2.08 million workers from the formal and informal sectors take unpaid leave or are laid off.

## Project Overview
The COMOT project is a web-based service application that connects people who are looking for work and companies, institutions or individuals who are looking for or opening new workforce recruitment.

## How To Run
- Open your terminal and write "npm install"
  
- Change config.json.example to config.json and fill with your pgadmin settings
  
- Change .env_example to .env and set it using mailtrap

- Run "node_modules/.bin/sequelize db:migrate" in your terminal
